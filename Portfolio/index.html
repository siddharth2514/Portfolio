Automatic Irrigation System Project


Project Overview: In collaboration with a team of four, we developed an automatic irrigation system utilizing Arduino technology. This project aimed to optimize water usage in agriculture by automating the irrigation process.


Technology Used:

  • Arduino: Central to our system, used for controlling and monitoring the irrigation process.
  • Sensors: Soil moisture sensors to detect soil moisture levels.
  • Actuators: Controlled water pumps to manage irrigation based on sensor data.


    • Functionality:

  • The system automatically waters plants when soil moisture levels fall below a predefined threshold.
  • It improves water efficiency and reduces manual labor in agricultural practices.

    • Project Significance: This project showcases our ability to apply IoT and automation technologies to solve real-world problems in agriculture.

    Smart Dustbin with Arduino, Motor, and Motion Sensor


    Project Overview: In collaboration with a team of two, we developed a Smart Dustbin using Arduino technology, a motor, and a motion sensor. Our objective was to create an innovative solution that automates waste disposal and promotes environmental sustainability.


    Technology Used:
  • Arduino: Microcontroller utilized for interfacing between components and controlling system behavior.
  • Motor: Actuator responsible for opening and closing the dustbin lid in response to motion detection.
  • Motion Sensor: Detects nearby motion, triggering the motorized lid mechanism.

    • Functionality:
  • The motion sensor detects approaching individuals, activating the motorized lid to open automatically.
  • Users can conveniently dispose of waste without physical contact, promoting hygiene and convenience.
  • After a predefined period of inactivity, the lid closes automatically, maintaining cleanliness and preventing odors.

    • Project Significance: Our collaborative effort in developing the Smart Dustbin showcases the application of Arduino-based automation to improve everyday waste management practices. By leveraging sensor technology and motorized mechanisms, we have created a solution that enhances user experience, promotes hygiene, and contributes to environmental sustainability.

    Airline Management System with SQL Database Integration


    Project Overview: In collaboration with a team of two, we developed an Airline Management System that integrates SQL databases for efficient data storage and retrieval. Our project aimed to streamline airline operations by providing a comprehensive platform for managing flights, reservations, and passenger information.


    Technology Used:
  • SQL Database: Utilized for storing and managing airline-related data, including flight schedules, passenger details, and reservation information.
  • Front-end Development: Implemented user interface components to interact with the SQL databases, enabling users to make changes and updates to the system.

    • Functionality:
  • Flight Management: The system allows airline staff to add, update, or cancel flights, including details such as departure/arrival times, destinations, and aircraft information.
  • Reservation System: Users can search for available flights, make reservations, and manage their booking details through the user-friendly interface.
  • Passenger Information: The system maintains records of passenger details, including contact information, seat assignments, and special requests.
